Submission Guidelines

Submission must be in PDF format (ONLY) and will be through the HotCRP portal. Check website for updates.


Papers: Submissions should be limited to six (6) pages including references. Every submitted paper will automatically be considered within poster submission pool, unless discouraged by the authors.
Poster and demo: Poster submissions must be in extended abstract format with 2 page limit (incl. refer- ences). Submission’s title must be prefixed with “Poster” or “Demo” accordingly. All submissions should comply with MobiSys guidelines . The reviewing process will be single-blind.
